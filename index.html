Restoration of decayed tooth.

1. Fillings - Fillings are done with light cure composites and glass ionomer cements of the best quality.

2. Root Canal Treatments - Extensively decayed teeth involving pulp require Root Canal Treatments and they are done with well researched and approved methods and materials.

Surgeries.

1. Removal of teeth - Simple,complicated,impacted teeth extractions are done with autoclaved and well maintained instruments and armamentarium.

2. Gum surgeries - Diseased gums are treated with researched and approved procedures

Replacement of teeth.

1. Removable partial or complete dentures - Quality labs and materials are assured.

2. Bridge - Tooth supported fixed type of replacement.Again quality assured.

3. Implants - Bone supported fixed type of replacement

Scaling.

Calculus and tartar removal to restore the health of gums supporting the teeth

Cosmetic treatments.

1. Veneers - Done for stained and teeth with flourosis
